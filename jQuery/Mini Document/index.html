Slide Down

Animates the height of the matched elements. This causes lower parts of the page to slide down, making way for the revealed items.

Slide Up

Animates the height of the matched elements. This causes lower parts of the page to slide up, making way for the revealed items.

Slide Toggle

causes lower parts of the page to slide up or down, appearing to reveal or conceal the items.

Fade In

animates the opacity of the matched elements.

Fade Out

animates the opacity of the matched elements. Once the opacity reaches 0, the display style property is set to none.

Before

After
